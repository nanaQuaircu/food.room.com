# HMS Feature Roadmap — PrineLuck Hotel PMS

Phased plan to close gaps against the full Hotel Management System specification.  
**Current baseline:** ~40% of enterprise HMS spec (strong single-property operations core).

---

## Phase 1 — Revenue & payments (4–6 weeks)

**Goal:** Complete the money loop for front desk and reservations.

| Feature | Effort | Status |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Paystack on front desk walk-in | 3–5 days | **Done** |
| Paystack on billing / check-out | 3–5 days | Planned |
| Rate plans (flexible, non-refundable, seasonal) | 1–2 weeks | Planned |
| Deposits at booking | 3–5 days | Planned |
| Tax rules engine | 1 week | Planned |
| Guest invoice / receipt PDF | 1 week | Planned |
| Night audit | 1–2 weeks | Planned |

## Recommended order

1. **Phase 1** — Paystack billing + rate plans + tax + invoices (highest ROI)
2. **Phase 2** — Calendar + booking emails (guest-facing quality)
3. **Phase 3** — Tape chart + POs + audit (operations maturity)
4. **Phase 4** — Booking engine + OTA (revenue growth)
5. **Phase 5+** — Enterprise scale
